How it works

Scan any product.
Know it's real.

Every product registered on VerifyChain gets a cryptographically signed QR code. Anyone — buyer, customs, regulator — scans it and sees the full chain of custody in under 2 seconds.

01
Seller registers a product batch — uploads origin, harvest/production date, photos. Gets a signed QR sheet to print.
02
QR tag is attached to each unit. Each tag is unique, encrypted, one-time-use.
03
Buyer scans with phone camera — no app needed. Sees full authenticity report instantly.
04
AI fraud detection flags suspicious patterns — same QR scanned in two countries, abnormal scan velocity, tampered tags.
